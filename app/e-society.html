Social Recruitment

Social recruitment process

★ Project manager, technical director, project principal

Requirements:
1. College degree or above in engineering management or civil engineering;
2. 5+ years of work experience on such positions, previously worked on the management of at least 1 complete project, with relevant certificates;
3. good English, could be used as the working language, aspiration to overseas development, accepting long term overseas work;
4. under the age of 50, strong skills on communication and coordination, teamwork awareness, sense of responsibility.


★ Safety officer, QC, construction officer, setting out operator, archive officer

Requirements:
1. College degree or above in engineering management or civil engineering or related majors, no major limitations for candidates with experience on similar positions;
2. 3+ years of work experience on such positions, relevant certificates for positions;
3. under the age of 45, good health, strong sense of responsibility, professionalism, could endure hardship.


★ Budget control/QS

Requirements:
1. College degree or above in engineering cost;
2. 3+ years of work experience on this position, related certificate, and a good English foundation;
3. familiar with the construction contract, budget quota and tender/bidding management of various professional projects, and could complete the budget and settlement clearance of the project independently;
4. under the age of 45, good health, strong sense of responsibility, strong communication and coordination skills.


★ Legal Affairs Manager

Requirements:
1. Bachelor degree or above in law-related major;
2. 5+ years of work experience on this position, more than 2 years of experience in the construction industry, and have relevant certificates;
3. familiar with the FIDIC terms, English could be used as a working language and can accept temporary overseas business trips;
4. under the age of 45, strong sense of responsibility, strong communication and coordination skills.


★ Human Resources

Requirements:
1. Bachelor degree or above in human resources, business administration, foreign language, Chinese, journalism, etc.;
2. 1+ year working experience on this position;
3. skillful operation of Office software, good English skill is preferred;
4. under the age of 35, strong communication and coordination skill, self-driven and strong execution ability.


★ Comprehensive administration

Requirements:
1. Bachelor degree or above in Chinese, journalism, computer, etc.;
2. 1+ year of work experience on this position, good English skill is preferred;
3. Skillful operation of office software, able of graphic design, good document writing ability;
4. under the age of 35, strong communication and coordination skill, self-driven and strong execution ability.


★ overseas financial

Requirements:
1. Bachelor degree or above in financial management, accounting or other related majors;
2. 2+ years of work experience on this position, proficient in financial software and office software;
3. English could be used as a working language, aspiration interest in overseas development, could accept long-term overseas work;
4. Under 45 years of age, good moral character, careful work with heart, and strong communication and coordination skills.
