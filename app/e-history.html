Development History

1983-1985

Taking use of partners’ resources, cooperating with large-scale、state-own enterprises，exporting labor services , and unveiling the curtain of going-out. Mainly focusing on Southeast Asia and the Middle East, the annual export of labor services was around 4,000 persons.

1995-2001

It has successively obtained the national labor cooperation license of overseas contracting project and project contracting franchise right, forming a regional layout with Singapore, Israel and Algeria as the core. The accumulated labor dispatched personnel are 17.3 thousand, and the turnover is 175.28 million US dollars.

2004-2015

Overseas operating strength has been continuously improved, and the development strategy has been gradually transformed and adjusted, established subordinate international engineering companies, overseas companies. In 2010, it entered the overseas aid market of the Ministry of Commerce. The international engineering contracting business developed rapidly.

2016.5.18

Zhongtong Grand Construction Group Limited was incorporated in Hong Kong with a registered capital of RMB 506.6 million. Nantong Sanjian Overseas Business Platform was integrated and established.

2016-2017

Vigorously promote the development of the "investment, financing and construction" model, and taking investment, merger and joint cooperation as a channel to develop and successfully had projects landing in Thailand, India, Malaysia, Serbia, Romania, and Namibia, etc.

2018.9

The strategic layout is optimized, and the resources are deeply concentrated, forming a “5+1+1” plan, i.e. “Southeast Asia + South Asia + Eastern Europe + South Africa + Middle East + overseas aid + expansion Area”, and the market is deeply cultivated.
