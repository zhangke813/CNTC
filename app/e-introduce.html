Under the background of economic globalization and “B&R (Belt and Road)” strategy strongly promoted by China government, Jiangsu Nantong Sanjian Construction Group Co., Ltd. integrated its existing resources in consistence with the situation to formally establish Zhong Tong Grand Construction Group Limited on August 6, 2015.


From its establishment, Zhong Tong Grand Construction Group Limited emphasizes global layout and insists on global operation with the supports of its brand, the core of talents and the highlight of projects for the main goals of bettering its international business, financial investment and leveraging competence so as to fully build a new investment and financing platform; moreover, it innovates its business mode in the path of internal promotion in order to realize harmonious multi-business development and enhance its operation capabilities over the whole industrial chain for the transition from“ going out” to“ going in and up”.


Bearing the core values of “build for a great future” in mind, Zhong Tong Grand Construction Group Limited insists on the development theory of “innovation, harmony, environmental protection, openness and sharing” and promotes Chinese “craftsman spirit” under the “made-in-China” symbol, while it actively utilizes advanced operation concepts and promotes the “macro infrastructure + others” mode to realize the economic transition from low-end operation to hi-end operation by means of “finance + others” , so CNTC can construct itself into an international company featured with “infrastructure expert”, ”B&R pioneer”, “internet + benchmark” and “model of production and financing”.


Zhong Tong Grand Construction stepping into the world will embrace marvelous prospects. Highlighting open mind and broad vision, it will always establish cooperation with all elites and excellent enterprises dedicated to embracing the world to jointly exploit the overseas market and create a bright future
